Our Process

We begin every renewable energy project with a detailed requirement evaluation to understand your energy needs and sustainability goals. After this, we handle all government licensing, ensuring full regulatory compliance before moving into the design and construction of customized solar or renewable energy systems.

From Testing to Partnership

Once built, each system undergoes thorough testing and commissioning to ensure peak performance. We finalize the project with a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA), establishing a long-term energy partnership that delivers reliable, clean power tailored to your needs.

  • Requirement Evaluation Understanding the client's renewable energy needs & goals
  • PPA Signing Signing the Power Purchase Agreement to proceed
  • Government Licensing Obtaining required permits and approvals
  • Design & Construction Executing the solar or renewable infrastructure setup
  • Testing & Commissioning Ensuring systems run efficiently before going live
  • Monitoring & Support Ongoing system monitoring and maintenance support
