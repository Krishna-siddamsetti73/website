About Us

Engineering India’s Green Future, One Watt at a Time

Creating for Future

At Sunanda Renewable Energy Pvt. Ltd., we are building the future of power — clean, sustainable, and smart.

At Sunanda Renewable Energy Pvt. Ltd., we are not just generating electricity — we are building a cleaner, greener, and smarter future. As a forward-thinking renewable energy company, our mission is to revolutionize how power is produced and delivered, enabling industries and communities to transition to sustainable energy effortlessly.

We specialize in clean energy generation through reliable, high-efficiency solar and wind power systems. What sets us apart is our zero-infrastructure model for clients — there’s no need to install solar panels or windmills on your premises. Through government-authorized transmission lines, we deliver green electricity directly to your site, eliminating the need for land, equipment, or operational hassle on your end.

From power purchase agreements (PPAs) and government compliance to generation, storage (BESS), and transmission, we handle the entire process with precision. Our goal is to ensure our clients receive uninterrupted, cost-effective, and environmentally responsible energy solutions, tailored to meet their specific power needs.

⚙ Our Execution Process

Our Process
We begin every renewable energy project with a detailed requirement evaluation to understand your energy needs and sustainability goals. After this, we handle all government licensing, ensuring full regulatory compliance before moving into the design and construction of customized solar or renewable energy systems.
From Testing to Partnership

Once built, each system undergoes thorough testing and commissioning to ensure peak performance. We finalize the project with a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA), establishing a long-term energy partnership that delivers reliable, clean power tailored to your needs.

  • Monitoring & Support Ongoing system monitoring and maintenance support
  • Testing & Commissioning Ensuring systems run efficiently before going live
  • Design & Construction Executing the solar or renewable infrastructure setup
  • Government Licensing Obtaining required permits and approvals
  • PPA Signing Signing the Power Purchase Agreement to proceed
  • Requirement Evaluation Understanding the client's renewable energy needs & goals
