Who We Are

Sunanda Renewable Energy Pvt. Ltd. is committed to driving the global shift toward clean, sustainable power. We believe in the future of renewable energy and specialize in generating electricity through reliable sources like solar power and wind energy.

Our energy transition model focuses on delivering clean power from our renewable energy plants directly to our clients via government-affiliated electrical lines, ensuring seamless and eco-friendly energy distribution.

Backed by a strong vision for a greener tomorrow, we empower individuals, industries, and communities to embrace renewable energy solutions that are both efficient and environmentally responsible.

Energy Storage System

BESS (Battery Energy Storage System) allows us to store excess energy generated from solar and wind sources during peak production hours and supply it when demand is high. This ensures consistent power delivery, supports grid stability, and helps clients enjoy uninterrupted green energy — day and night.
